Miichigo Chair

The Miichigo Chair is a low-profile meditation device built with the intention of re-connecting with our inner-child. The design took inspiration from the fireworks visual effect when Remy took his bite of good food in Ratatouille. For me, meditation is a way to slow down and savour the delicious bite of life.
